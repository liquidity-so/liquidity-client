Do I cancel my subscription when I want?

Yes, you can  change or cancel your plan at any time in your dashboard. Once you have canceled, you’ll still have access to your premium plan until the end of your subscription cycle.

Can I share my subscription with my team?

Yes! You are free to add team members to share your subscription with. In this case, all the team members will share the common monthly quota allowed by the subscriptions.

If a request returns no result, does it count?

For a trade simulation, a request is counted each time you click buy or sell. It does not matter how many exchanges are returned.
